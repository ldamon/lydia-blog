Hi! I'm a comedian and writer. I'm originally from Boston, well Dedham, Mass. where I grew up in a house built in 1810 across the street from a cemetery. Spooky!

I went to Trinity College in Hartford, CT, where I studied Art History and waited in many lines outside of frat basements. Degrading!

I moved to the Bay Area to Make It Big as a #WomanInTech, but then realized I'd rather be a "Woman In [something else]" and started performing standup comedy on shows like Comedy Oakland and Don't Tell Comedy. My writing draws on my experience as a designer for tech startups, pursuing comedy and writing, and being an insecure, sometimes overconfident lady on a journey to become...a woman in something.
