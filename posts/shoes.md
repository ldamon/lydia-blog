I have no idea why, but I woke up this mornign thinking about these shoes.

![Orange heels](shoes.png)

## Dressed up for what exactly?

I brought these shoes to my sophomore year of college. I intended to wear them "out" AKA  to "late night" AKA the frats. Now will someone please explain to me why I wore shoes made for a bridal shower brunch to a sludge covered frat basement? All I can think is it was some kind of Blair Waldorfian influence. 

![Queen of bows](blair.jpg)

She was wearing a lot of bows and bow headbands, and I guess my thought was: let me try that, but on my feet? In the winter in Hartford, Connecticut? When we had to walk from our dorm across campus to the frats, and teeter down a basement staircase in the hopes that a brother would admit us so we could grind to Sean Kingston until 2am? 

More on this later as the story develops.
